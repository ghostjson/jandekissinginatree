ABOUT US

How We Met

We met in the late Summer of August 2018 at our best friends' wedding. I was a bridesmaid and he was the best man. I remember him walking through the Church doors for rehearsal and thinking he had the most beautiful skin. He kept giving me covert glances throughout the rehearsal. We got a chance to sit together at the wedding reception and started to talk and really hit it off. We had our first dance at the same venue as our now current reception.

We exchanged numbers and video called every night when he went back home. I visited Bermuda shortly after and we made it official. For a year we dated while going back and forth. During that time we both realized this was it for the both of us. This was my ‘mutual weirdness forever’. We just fit. And now here we are… and so our adventure begins…
