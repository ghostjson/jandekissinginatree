ABOUT US

How We Met

We met in the fall of 2010 working at Freshly Squeezed. There were two back to back locations at the Eaton's Center and although we worked in different stores we found every reason to greet each other whenever we bumped into one another. One year during the holidays, the managers threw a Christmas party for both stores and that's when we really connected.


He had the same weird sense of humour as me and we never had a problem making each other laugh. We started talking to each other more and more, sometimes even using our coworkers as an excuse to go out and eat. In April 2011 as we grew closer, the little bugger finally found the courage to ask me to be his girlfriend. The rest is history :)
