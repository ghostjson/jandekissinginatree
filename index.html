ABOUT US

How We Met

We met in the late Summer of August 2018 at 2 of our best friends weddings. I was a bridesmaid and he was the best man. I remember him walking through the Church doors for rehearsal and thinking he had the most beautiful dark skin. He kept giving me covert glances throughout the rehearsal. We got a chance to sit together at the wedding reception and started to talk and really hit it off. We had our first dance at the same venue as our now current reception. We exchanged numbers and talked every night on video call when he went back home. 2 months later while visiting Bermuda, he asked me to be his girl and to make it official.

For a year we dated while going back and forth and during that time we both realized this was it for the both of us. This was my ‘mutual weirdness forever’. We just fit. He had the same humour as me and we never had a problem making each other laugh. He loved me and was never afraid to say it or show it (and vice versa). On January 11, a year and 5 months later, he put a beautiful picnic together at secluded beach, got down on 1 knee and asked me do the honour of being his wife. And so our adventure begins…
